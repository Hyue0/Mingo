Understanding Mental Health

It includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects the way we feel, act, and think. If we lack a positive mental health, it can lead to several complications in our life.

What Causes Mental Illnesses?

Possible causes may include:

Childhood trauma, history of abuse, chronic illness, biological factors, chemical imbalance in the brain, stress, etc.

Why Care?

Like our physical health, mental health has a significant contribution to our overall health which affects all aspects of our faily life. For instance, studies have shown that depression leads to an increased chance of diabetes, heart disease, and stroke. These chronic illnesses can also lead to a higher risk of mental illness. Several researchers have been done that those with a healthier mind correlates to better overall health.
