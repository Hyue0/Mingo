MINGO ━━━ ABOUT US

As high school students in computer science, we recognize the profound impact of mental health on our community and our peers. Understanding that mental well-being is integral to academic success and personal development, we care deeply about creating a supportive environment for everyone. By making a website about mental health, we aim to leverage technology to destigmatize discussions around mental well-being and provide valuable resources. Our platform seeks to raise awareness, fostering empathy and understanding among our peers. We believe that a mentally healthy community is more conducive to collaboration and innovation, critical elements in our field. Ultimately, our initiative is not just about coding; it's about using our skills to contribute positively to the well-being of our fellow students and building a compassionate and resilient community.
